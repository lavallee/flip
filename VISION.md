# Vision — flip

**North star:** Research survives context windows, handoffs, tools, and years
because the knowledge bundle at rest can show not only what it claims, but what
was captured, how it was judged, which decisions followed, and where uncertainty
remains.

**North-star metric:** reusable verified claims — load-bearing claims that meet
their profile's corroboration bar and are successfully picked up in a later
session or by a different human/agent without reconstructing the source trail.

## Strategy bets

- **Plain knowledge bundles are the durable interface.** Native OKF markdown
  pages, stable IDs, append-only events, and generated views outlive any one
  application or agent runtime.
- **Lineage is the differentiator.** Source custody, explicit grading,
  corroboration-gated claims, logged generation, and negative evidence turn a
  wiki into a notebook that can justify reliance.
- **Humans and agents edit the same artifact.** Key-preserving round trips,
  Obsidian affordances, CLI checks, and portable skills support collaboration
  without parallel sources of truth.

## Non-goals

- A hosted research database, retrieval service, or proprietary workspace.
- Automatically declaring synthesized or retrieved material to be evidence.
- Owning fetchers, editors, renderers, or publishing platforms.
- Replacing judgment with metadata completeness or a composite trust score.

## Engine map

- OKF notebook and beat manifests define the portable at-rest contract.
- Entity pages hold sources, claims, decisions, questions, and sessions; event
  ledgers preserve provenance and negative evidence.
- The CLI enforces custody, grading, corroboration, migration, export, and
  generated-view invariants.
- Obsidian integration gives humans a native editing surface; Spindle skills
  teach agents the same lineage discipline.
- BagIt, CSL, and policy-filtered OKF exports connect notebooks to other
  archival and publishing systems without changing the canonical files.
